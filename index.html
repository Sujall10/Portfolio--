FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Why wouldn't I hire a full-time designer? +
Hiring a full-time designer can be costly, especially if you don't have a continuous stream of design work. By using an unlimited design agency, you can access a team of designers for a fraction of the cost of a full-time hire. Additionally, with an agency, you benefit from a diverse range of skills and expertise.
How many requests can I have? +
The number of requests you can have depends on the agency's specific pricing model. Typically, agencies offer different tiers based on the number of simultaneous requests or the complexity of the projects. It's important to choose a plan that aligns with your anticipated workload.
What programs do you use? +
We use a variety of industry-standard design programs, such as Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign), Sketch, Figma, and others, depending on the specific needs of the project.
What if I don't like the design? +
Unlimited design agencies often offer revisions or iterations to ensure that the final design meets your expectations. If you're not satisfied with the initial design, you can provide feedback, and the designers will make the necessary adjustments until you're happy with the result.
